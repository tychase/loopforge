For research, I’d start with Self-Determination Theory and flow, then use variable-ratio reinforcement mostly as a warning label, not the heart of the game. SDT gives you a clean positive framework: autonomy, competence, and relatedness. Variable reward research is useful, but because of its overlap with gambling-like systems, the agent should treat it as ethically sensitive.
